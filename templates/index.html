Madurai waterbodies

Ponds & Ooranis

Ponds, locally known as Oorani, are small water bodies typically used for domestic purposes, livestock, and community needs in rural areas. They play a crucial role in recharging groundwater and providing water during dry seasons.

Temple Tanks

Temple tanks are sacred water reservoirs associated with temples, used for religious rituals and community events.

Irrigation Tanks

Irrigation tanks are larger water bodies designed for agricultural purposes, helping to store rainwater and provide irrigation through an interconnected network of channels.
